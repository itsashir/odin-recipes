Burger

Description

Burger is a popular sandwich fast food consists of cooked patty placed between two halves of a bun. Often topped with ingredients like lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickle
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cook the patty for about 3-4 minutes
  2. Lightly toast the inside of the buns
  3. Spread mayonnaise on the bun
  4. Add lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles
  5. Place the cooked patty with cheese on top
  6. Top with the other bun half
  7. Serve hot with fries and enjoy
