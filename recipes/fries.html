Fries

Description

Fries are thin sticks or wedges of deep-fried or baked potatoes, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Typically seasoned with salt but can be flavored with various spices or toppings.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Spices (Optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin sticks or wedges
  2. Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for at least 30 minutes
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry
  4. Heat oil to 300°F and fry the potatoes for 4-5 minutes
  5. Raise temp to 375°F and fry again for 2-3 minutes
  6. While still hot sprinkle some salt (and any spices of choice)
  7. Serve with ketchup or any dip
